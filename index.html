McDegens DAO Whitepaper


Who are the McDegens?
Despite only making up 0.38% of the 350k Faceless population, a workforce of 1,345 McDegens are instantly recognisable within the 350k Faceless collection. Wearing iconic uniforms of red and gold headwear and outfits, McDegens easily stand out from the crowd.

As Tensor opened its marketplace to trade The Faceless collection, McDegens quickly attracted a premium and a subculture was born. This community rallied behind the McDegens traits – but with cNFTs being such a new technology, obvious gaps in the ecosystem were quickly discovered.

It took only a matter of hours before devs and community members came together to establish the McDegens DAO – a fun place where kindness, respect and hard work was appreciated, and new tools and apps were built rapidly to address the shortcomings of the existing ecosystem.

In doing so a passionate community was fired up and stoked, and the McDegens discord has been a thriving hub of productivity, friendship and laughter ever since.

What is the McDegens DAO?
McDegens DAO is a decentralized autonomous organization committed to providing its members with innovative tools and dApps centred around NFTs and cNFTs. Our vision is to empower the NFT community, artists, and collectors by creating a vibrant ecosystem that enhances the utility, accessibility, and value of NFTs.

By leveraging blockchain technology and community governance, McDegensDAO aims to foster collaboration, creativity, and decentralized decision-making within the NFT space. We strive to address the evolving needs and challenges faced by NFT enthusiasts, offering cutting-edge solutions that promote inclusivity, fairness, and the exploration of new possibilities.

Through our dApps, McDegensDAO seeks to unlock the true potential of NFTs and cNFTs, enabling our members to discover, trade, and interact with digital assets in exciting and meaningful ways. We are dedicated to building a robust infrastructure that supports the growth of the NFT ecosystem while providing ample opportunities for artists to showcase their creations and collectors to engage with unique digital artifacts.

With a strong commitment to community-driven governance, McDegens DAO operates with a council of five members who represent the interests of the community. Community members possess full voting powers and actively participate in shaping the direction and initiatives of the DAO. Proposals and ideas put forth by the community are reviewed and executed by the council, ensuring that decisions are aligned with the collective vision and preferences of the members.

The overarching mission of McDegens DAO is to foster an environment where the NFT community can thrive, collaborate, and explore new horizons. We envision a future where NFTs are widely embraced as a transformative medium, empowering creators and collectors worldwide. Through our collective efforts, McDegens DAO aims to revolutionize the NFT landscape and create lasting value for its members.
